Let me introduce you to Demon Duck Hunt the Game!
Its fairly simple, all you have to do is click on the demons and shoot them! Make sure they cant get near any of your crops.
The goal is, shoot as many as you can and try to progress on to the next level! For level 2 you need to shoot 7 demons, for level 3 you need 20 and to beat the game you need 40
Best of luck out there partner! Happy Hunting
