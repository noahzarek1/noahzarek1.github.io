Let me introduce you to Hoppy Clicker!
Its fairly simple, all you have to do is click on the right side of the screen and gain hoppys! Can you beat each quest to take over the world?
The goal is, get the highest HPS(hops per second) and HPC(hops per click) that you can get! This program is heavily inspired by Cookie Clicker, it is a simple afk clicker game made in khan Academy.
